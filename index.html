Add to iPad home screen

Follow these steps to install this app on your iPad so it opens like a native app — no browser chrome, just the calculator.

  1. Open this page in Safari on your iPad (it must be Safari, not Chrome)
  2. Tap the Share button at the top of Safari — it looks like a box with an arrow pointing up
  3. Scroll down in the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. You can rename it — "Golf Bets" works well — then tap Add
  5. The ⛳ icon now appears on your home screen. Tap it to open the app fullscreen with no browser bar.
Add to iPhone home screen

Same steps work on iPhone — the app layout adjusts automatically to the smaller screen.

You can share the same HTML file to anyone in the group — they follow the same steps on their own device.
